Chicken soup

The Western chicken soup

  • Chicken broth
  • Carrot
  • Fresh chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  1. Pour one can of chicken broth into pot and cook for 10 minutes
  2. Cut all the ingredients into small pieces

  3. Add all ingredients into the pot and add 100 mL of water

    4. Heat for 1 hours and ready to serve
